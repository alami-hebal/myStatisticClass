PRACTICE LIST (MUST-COMPLETE SECTION)

This section is important and must be completed by participating volunteer students. In the Practice List section, students are required to complete all 8 tasks. Please review each item carefully, watch the accompanying videos, and complete the assigned tasks. All tasks must be completed within two weeks of receiving access to this website link.

VIDEO LESSON (OPTIONAL SECTION)

The "Video Lesson" section features engaging, beginner-friendly tutorials aimed at helping students grasp fundamental statistical concepts. These videos simplify topics such as statistical data, graph interpretation, data description and data analysis. Students can access these videos alongside the tasks provided in the Practice List section.

The "Infographic Dashboard" section presents visual data tools designed to enhance cognitive understanding and support metacognitive skills. It encourages students to monitoring their performance, analyze patterns, reflect on insights and self-regulated engagement with statistical information.
