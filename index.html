WHAT IS THIS WEB PAGE ABOUT?

This web page is developed to create an online learning environment designed to study student behavior and activity during online classes. It involves setting up systems to monitor, record and analyze how students engage with their learning and interact with their peers. The primary objective of this research is to investigate students’ cognitive and metacognitive learning behaviors with a focus on self-reflection through both qualitative and quantitative analyses.

This grant-funded research is conducted under the School of Distance Education at Universiti Sains Malaysia (USM), with Dr. Nurulhuda Binti Ramli serving as the project leader. Approximately 40 or more selected students will be invited to explore this webpage, complete tasks listed in the Practice List, and monitor their progress through a personalized dashboard. Participation in the Practice List is entirely voluntary.

Although the learning tasks are aligned with the course materials, they will not contribute to students’ coursework marks. Students will be fully informed about the study's purpose, procedures, and their rights, including the freedom to participate or decline. Their privacy will be safeguarded by ensuring all data remains confidential, particularly in any publications. Participants will be required to sign a consent form before joining the study and will receive a small token of appreciation amount RM20 for their involvement.




INTRODUCTION

COGNITIVE

Cognitive refers to mental processes involved in gaining knowledge and understanding. It includes functions such as thinking, learning, memory, problem-solving, and attention. Cognitive skills help individuals process information, make decisions, and apply knowledge in different situations, playing a key role in learning and development.

METACOGNITIVE

Metacognitive refers to the awareness and understanding of one’s own thinking and learning processes. It involves planning, monitoring, and evaluating how effectively you learn or solve problems. Metacognitive skills help individuals adjust strategies, stay focused, and improve learning outcomes through self-reflection and regulation of their cognitive activities.

SELF REGULATED LEARNING (SRL)

Self-regulated learning is a process where learners take active control of their learning by setting goals, managing time, staying motivated, and reflecting on their progress. It involves cognitive and metacognitive factors to plan, monitor, and evaluate learning for better academic outcomes.

THE IMPORTANCE AND BENEFITS OF RESEARCH

THRIVING IS ALL ABOUT WORKING ON YOUR SELF-DEVELOPMENT

The profiling of students' cognitive and metacognitive can be used by educators to improve self-regulation learning instructions and provide training for students using different strategies. This is particularly important in online learning settings where reflective practices can be supported effectively. Learning analytics methods can help students regulate their learning process and improve their reflective practice through metacognitive, ultimately leading to more successful outcomes. The findings of students' cognitive and metacognitive behavior profiles can also be used to develop data-driven software for online teaching and learning, addressing challenges in the education sector.

Finally, the cognitive and metacognitive behavioural profile in online environment can be replicated and validated by other researchers for other groups of higher education samples and through different online learning settings in future research.

Virtual training systems

An efficient virtual training system refers to a platform or software that effectively delivers training and educational content in a virtual or online setting. This system include user-friendly interfaces, interactive prompt assistant elements, reliable performance analytics, collaboration tools, scalability prediction score, and flexibility to adapt.

Online remote monitoring

Online remote monitoring refers to the process of monitoring and supervising activities, processes, or systems from a remote location using internet-connected devices or technology. This practice allows individuals or organizations to track and gather data in real-time without physically being present at the location of interest.

Web Class Analysis

Web class analysis refers to the evaluation and assessment of online courses. It involves analyzing various aspects such as content quality, cognitive, metacognitive, learning outcomes, student score performance, and overall effectiveness of the virtual instruction. By examining these factors, educators and administrators could predict future results using using machine learning.

SAMPLE CLASS

The focus of the study is directed toward mathematics students enrolled at School of Distance Education, Universiti Sains Malaysia (USM). Cognitive and metacognitive behaviours will be assessed utilizing online behavioural analytics tools

Web Analytics Tools & Analyzing Benefit

Web analytics refers to the measurement, collection, analysis, and reporting of web data to understand and optimize the usage of a website. It involves tracking various metrics and user interactions on a website to assess its performance, user behavior, and effectiveness in achieving business goals. Since strength analytic tools is on website so classroom by website based will be proposed in this research. A variety of machine learning regression algorithms will be employed to forecast learning behaviours and performance outcomes. The overarching objective is to establish a robust profiling framework that facilitates precise data collection, algorithm selection, and insightful analysis.

Web analytics tools provide insights into key performance indicators (KPIs) such as website traffic, user demographics, click-through rates, conversion rates, bounce rates, and more. By analyzing these metrics, website owners and marketers can make informed decisions to improve the user experience, optimize conversion rates, enhance online marketing strategies, and ultimately achieve their objectives. For this research HOTJAR, MICROSOFT CLARITY and GOOGLE ANALYTIC will used as analysis tools.

Web analytics helps in identifying areas for improvement, understanding user preferences, and measuring the success of digital marketing campaigns. It plays a crucial role in shaping website content, design, and functionality to drive traffic, engage users, and maximize the overall impact of an online presence. Presentation by graph, heatmap and recording video will give right monitoring experience. These findings are likely to advance educational techniques, aid in customizing learning paths for students, and strengthen institutional choices to enhance learning results.
