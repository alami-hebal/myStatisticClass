  • By: Dato’ Shaari Abdul Rahman

This paper highlights the basic requirements in being a statistician, the attributes of a competent statistician and the image of the profession. It also dwells on whether statisticians in the Malaysian civil service meet the competency criteria as expected by the public service, more so, in handling the unexpected and the unpredictable in today’s world scenario.
