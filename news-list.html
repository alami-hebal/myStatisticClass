  • Posted: 27/11/2024
  • By: Dato’ Shaari Abdul Rahman

This paper highlights the basic requirements in being a statistician, the attributes of a competent statistician and the image of the profession. It also dwells on whether statisticians in the Malaysian civil service meet the competency criteria as expected by the public service, more so, in handling the unexpected and the unpredictable in today’s world scenario. In terms of the relevancy of the service and strategies in making the service more relevant in the public services as well as the effectiveness in delivering excellent services, this paper delves further by explaining the role of statisticians in narrowing the gap of uncertainty in planning with providing the relevant information.
